The opportunity for Taiwanese youth

Experience local American culture with Quincy Davis.

The Summer Immersion truly offers an in-depth look at the people and culture of San Diego. This is a great way to learn a new culture, meet new people, grow in cultural understanding, while helping others through sports and other social activities.

Explore knowledge through sports and science.

Focusing in the principles of Teaching, participants in our academy should grow in an understanding of global solidarity, working others through sports, the dignity of the human person, for underserved populations, as well as growing in their own identity and role as a global citizen.

Traveling with professional teacher and leader.

We have the best teachers and leader Quincy Davis traveling with your kids. Through daily activities, students will be given the opportunity to socialize with one another and see what Southern California has to offer. Each activity will help to improve the students' English communication skills and cultural competency.
